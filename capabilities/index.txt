Capabilities
============

Neo4j is an enterprise graph database  on performance, robustness and scalability providing features such as:

* True ACID transactions with JTA support.
* High availability (see the High Availability chapter).
* Scales to billion of nodes and relationships on single machine hardware.
* High speed traversals and querying of the data.
